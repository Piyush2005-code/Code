NeetCode 150 - 15 Day Internship Interview Crash Plan

Objective

Complete exposure to all major interview patterns in 15 days.

Target:

* 30-35 questions/day
* Solve Easy problems completely
* Spend 20-30 minutes attempting Mediums
* If stuck, study solution and write it again without looking
* Maintain a pattern notebook

⸻

Rules

For each question:

1. Read problem.
2. Think for 10 minutes.
3. If no idea, watch/read solution.
4. Close solution.
5. Re-implement from memory.
6. Write pattern learned in notes.

Do NOT spend:

* 2 hours on one Medium.
* Entire day on one Hard.

Exposure > Perfection.

Interview Strategy

If asked a question:

1. Clarify input/output.
2. Give brute force.
3. Analyze complexity.
4. Improve step-by-step.
5. Talk continuously.

Interviewers often evaluate communication and reasoning more than whether you instantly know the optimal solution.

Pattern recognition wins internship interviews.

One more thing: based on the N-Queens code you showed, I would spend extra time on Trees, Graphs, and Backtracking. Your recursive thinking is already stronger than your DP and implementation maturity, so you can get interview-ready faster by leaning into those strengths rather than trying to become a DP expert in 15 days.
